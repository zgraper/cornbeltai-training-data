Explicit weakness checks
  - Too many clean queries: yes; only 36 vague rows and 2 short rows in the baseline set.
  - Lack of short queries (1-5 words): yes; 2 rows total.
  - Lack of vague queries: yes; 36 rows matched vague heuristics.
  - Lack of multi-topic queries: yes; 275 rows total.
  - Edge routing combinations:
  - weather_only: 79
  - web_only: 131
  - farm_only: 71
  - rag_plus_weather: 0
  - rag_plus_farm: 339
  - web_plus_weather: 0
  - Weak non-ag coverage: ratio is 30.0%, but many negatives were templated and low-diversity.

Changes applied
---------------
Rewrote existing rows: 200 total (160 non-ag, 40 ag).
Added new rows: 400 total, including at least 100 hard/adversarial examples.
Added coverage for short queries, messy phrasing, vague questions, mixed-intent prompts, and missing routing combinations.
